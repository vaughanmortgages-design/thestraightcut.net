Buying Guide

How to choose.

Comfort and audio beat flashy extras.

Buying Tip

Headset before graphics

Positional audio changes how you play more than most cosmetic upgrades.

Buying Tip

Storage fills fast

Modern titles are huge — plan for an expansion drive early.

Buying Tip

Wired for competitive

Wired controllers and mice cut latency for serious play.

Buying Tip

Pre-owned for back catalogue

eBay is the best route to older consoles and out-of-print games.
