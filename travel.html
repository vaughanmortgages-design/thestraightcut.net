Frequently asked questions

Before you book.

How should I compare a hotel and a vacation rental?

Compare location, space, kitchen needs, check-in logistics and the full terms shown by the booking provider. The Straight Cut does not publish unverified prices or policies.

Does The Straight Cut set hotel or rental prices?

No. Prices, availability and booking terms are controlled by the travel partner and should be confirmed on its website.

Which partner is used for luxury travel?

Skylark Travel Group is the approved premium and luxury travel partner in the Affiliate Link Vault.

Where can I find vacation homes?
