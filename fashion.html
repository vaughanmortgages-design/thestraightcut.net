Buying Guide

How to choose.

Staples first, statement second.

Buying Tip

Build around neutrals

A few neutral staples mix into far more outfits than trend pieces.

Buying Tip

Fit over label

A well-fitting basic looks better than an ill-fitting designer piece.

Buying Tip

Invest in footwear

Shoes and a good jacket are worn most — spend there.

Buying Tip

Care extends life

Wash cold, hang dry and rotate to keep clothes looking new.
