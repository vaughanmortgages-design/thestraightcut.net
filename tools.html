Buying Guide

How to choose.

Start with a platform, then add to it.

Buying Tip

Commit to one battery

Pick a cordless brand and buy bare tools that share its batteries.

Buying Tip

Buy the drill and driver

A drill/driver plus an impact driver covers most household jobs.

Buying Tip

Storage keeps you fast

A chest or organized bag saves more time than another gadget.

Buying Tip

Never skip safety

Glasses, gloves and hearing protection are the cheapest tools you’ll own.
