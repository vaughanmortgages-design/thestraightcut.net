Buying Guide

How to choose.

Fix friction points before buying decor.

Buying Tip

Solve the daily annoyance

The best upgrade is usually the thing you trip over or search for every day.

Buying Tip

Layer the lighting

A lamp or two beats one overhead light for making a room feel finished.

Buying Tip

Storage that you can reach

Bins and shelves only help if the things you use most stay within arm’s reach.

Buying Tip

Buy washable

Removable, washable covers keep bedding and cushions looking new far longer.
