Buying Guide

How to choose.

Make reading easy to reach.

Buying Tip

Mix formats

Print for keepers, e-reader for travel and late-night reading.

Buying Tip

Follow a series or author

Back-catalogue titles are cheap and keep the momentum going.

Buying Tip

Light it well

A clip light or good lamp makes long reading comfortable.

Buying Tip

Used for value

eBay and used listings are great for out-of-print and bargain finds.
