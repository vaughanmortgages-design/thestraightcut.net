Buying Guide

How to choose.

Protect first, then personalise.

Buying Tip

Case + glass on day one

The cheapest insurance for a phone is a case and a tempered-glass protector fitted before first use.

Buying Tip

Watch charging standards

Match your phone’s fast-charge standard so a new brick actually charges quickly.

Buying Tip

Tablets for media and notes

A mid-range tablet handles streaming, reading and note-taking without laptop pricing.

Buying Tip

Unlocked saves long term

Unlocked handsets avoid carrier lock-in and are easy to resell.
